How to parry

In any installment of The Legend of Zelda series, executing a parry move requires precision and timing.

  1. First, equip your shield by pressing the corresponding button or using the assigned item slot.
  2. When facing an enemy's attack, keep a close eye on their movements and the moment just before their strike connects.
  3. As the enemy's weapon is about to hit you, press the shield button at the right time.
